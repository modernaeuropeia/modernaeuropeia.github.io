About Us

At Moderna Europeia, we combine cutting-edge technology with deep industry experience to deliver innovative IT solutions. From backend systems to embedded applications, we pride ourselves on transforming your vision into reality.

Meet the Team

Pedro Lopes

Pedro is a versatile Software Engineer with several years of experience across industries such as satellite navigation, energy, IoT, embedded systems, and graphics programming. His expertise lies in backend development and real-time system simulations, with a deep passion for building optimized, high-performance software.

Pedro is currently working as a full-stack developer, focusing on building GUI and systems for synthetic analysis using Python, PyQt, and scientific libraries. His recent work emphasizes developing robust, scalable software for high-performance environments.

Throughout his career, Pedro has been part of key projects like the development of a GNSS signal simulator for the Galileo satellite constellation at GMV, where he led efforts to optimize signal generation using C++ and multithreading techniques.

In the energy sector, Pedro played a significant role at Smart Energy Lab, building IoT products using Python and Rust, and contributed to hardware and firmware development for energy projects.

Pedro is constantly working on personal projects, including a variety of games, 2D/3D rendering engines, and software tools. You can explore his work on his GitHub.
